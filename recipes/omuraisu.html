Omuraisu

Omuraisu is a japanese meal that incorporates rice, ketchup and of course an egg.

Additionally, you can add chicken and onions to the meal, or any other vegetable for that matter.

Heres how to make it:

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Ketchup
  • Egg (Ideally 2)

Instructions

  1. Mix cold cooked rice with a bit of ketchup.
  2. Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook eggs in a pan, fold in half.
  4. Fill with rice, fold over.
  5. Drizzle with ketchup.
  6. Serve and enjoy your Omuraisu!
