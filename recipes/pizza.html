Pizza

Pizza is an all-time favourite dish that has spread across the world, in every variation it always maintains the basic formation of having a crust, sauce and toppings.

We will stick to the basics, we will make a simple cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated Cheese

Instructions

  1. Spread pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  2. Add tomato sauce and toppings.
  3. Bake until crust is golden.
  4. Slice and enjoy your pizza!
